<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Neo-Brutalist Luxury / High-Tech Noir**
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Unapologetic Authority**: The design should feel heavy, substantial, and confident. No soft edges or apologetic whitespace.
  2.  **Data as Art**: The system's primary output is data (leads, revenue). This data should be visualized as beautiful, complex artifacts.
  3.  **The "Black Box" Mystique**: Emphasize the hidden, powerful nature of the AI. It's a machine you feed, and it produces gold.
  4.  **Industrial Elegance**: Think high-end server racks, machined metal, and laser-etched glass.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **Void Black (#050505)**: The canvas. Infinite depth.
  *   **Alchemical Gold (#F7E7CE)**: Used sparingly for high-value data points and primary actions. Represents the "gold" produced by the alchemy.
  *   **Terminal Green (#00FF41)**: For system status, code snippets, and "live" data streams. A nod to the underlying code.
  *   **Electric Violet (#8A2BE2)**: For the "magic" moments—AI processing, neural network visualizations.

  **Layout Paradigm**: **The Dashboard as Hero**. Instead of a traditional landing page with sections, the entire site feels like a live, operating system.
  *   **Split Screen**: One side is the "Terminal" (scrolling logs, code), the other is the "View" (3D visualizations, marketing copy).
  *   **Grid-Breaking Overlays**: Modal windows and data panels that float over the main content, creating depth.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **The Gravity Well**: A WebGL visualization of leads being pulled into a central point (revenue).
  2.  **Live Logs**: A scrolling ticker of "system activity" (simulated) to show the machine is working.
  3.  **Glassmorphism 2.0**: heavily blurred, frosted glass panels with very thin, sharp borders.

  **Interaction Philosophy**: **Tactile & Mechanical**. Buttons should feel like physical switches. Hover effects should trigger "system diagnostics" or data reveals.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Glitch & Scanlines**: Subtle, high-tech interference effects on text and images.
  *   **Data Flow**: Lines of light connecting different parts of the UI.
  *   **Instant Snap**: Transitions should be fast and precise, not floaty.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Headings**: **Instrument Serif** (Italicized). Elegant, sharp, and slightly dangerous.
  *   **Body/Data**: **Geist Mono**. Technical, legible, and code-like.
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: **Cyber-Industrial / Brutalist Utility**
  **Core Principles**:
  1.  **Raw Functionality**: Exposed structure. Grid lines, technical markings, raw data.
  2.  **System Transparency**: Show the "guts" of the machine.
  3.  **Monochromatic Power**: Use a single color (e.g., red or orange) to dominate.
  4.  **Information Density**: Pack as much data as possible into the view.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  *   **Concrete Grey (#333333)**: The structural base.
  *   **Safety Orange (#FF4500)**: For alerts, actions, and highlights.
  *   **Blueprint Blue (#0047AB)**: For technical diagrams and schematics.

  **Layout Paradigm**: **The Modular Grid**. Everything is in a box. Borders are thick and visible.
  *   **Bento Box**: A grid of cards, each containing a specific piece of information.
  *   **Sidebar Navigation**: A persistent, tool-heavy sidebar.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1.  **Barcode/QR Code Motifs**: Used as decorative elements.
  2.  **Technical Crosshairs**: Custom cursor and hover effects.
  3.  **Monospaced Everything**: Even headings use a monospaced font.

  **Interaction Philosophy**: **Precise & Clicky**. Interactions should feel like operating a piece of heavy machinery.

  **Animation**:
  *   **Typewriter Effect**: Text appears character by character.
  *   **Slide & Reveal**: Panels slide out to reveal content.
  *   **Blinking Cursors**: Everywhere.

  **Typography System**:
  *   **Headings**: **Space Mono**. Bold, technical.
  *   **Body**: **Space Mono**. Consistent, raw.
</idea>
