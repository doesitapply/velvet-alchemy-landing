# Velvet Alchemy - Operations Manual

**For:** The person running the daily business  
**Purpose:** Turn terrible restaurant websites into $5k paychecks  
**Time Required:** 2-3 hours per day

---

## What This Business Actually Does

You find restaurants with ugly, broken websites. You prove their website is losing them money using AI. Then you sell them a new website for $3,000-$8,000.

**The Magic:** Everything is automated. The AI does the hard work. You just need to follow the system.

---

## Your Daily Routine (The Money Loop)

### Morning (30 minutes): Find New Leads

**Step 1:** Open the Command Center  
Go to: `https://your-website.com/command-center`

**Step 2:** Click "Go to Business Scraper"

**Step 3:** Enter a city and search term
- City: "Reno, NV" (or any city you're targeting)
- Search: "restaurants" or "bars" or "cafes"
- Click "Scrape Businesses"

**What happens:** The system finds 20 businesses from Google Maps and saves them to your database.

**Step 4:** Go back to Command Center  
You should now see "20 leads" in the stats.

---

### Mid-Morning (1 hour): Run Audits

**Step 1:** Click "Go to Orchestrator"

**Step 2:** Click the big "Run Pipeline" button at the top

**What happens:** The AI visits each restaurant's website, takes a screenshot, analyzes what's wrong, generates marketing assets, and calculates a "Prestige Score" (0-100).

**Wait time:** 5-10 minutes for all 20 audits to complete.

**Step 3:** Review the results
- Look for restaurants with **Prestige Scores between 40-70**
- Too low (under 40) = they probably can't afford you
- Too high (over 70) = they don't need you
- Sweet spot (40-70) = they have money but their website sucks

---

### Afternoon (1 hour): Send Outreach

**Step 1:** Click "Go to Charmer" from Command Center

**Step 2:** You'll see a list of audited restaurants

**Step 3:** Click on a restaurant with a score of 40-70

**Step 4:** Review the AI-generated email
- The AI writes a personalized pitch based on the audit
- It mentions specific problems it found on their website
- It offers a free consultation

**Step 5:** Click "Send Email" (or copy the email and send manually)

**Goal:** Send 5-10 outreach emails per day.

---

### When Someone Responds (The Sales Call)

**Step 1:** Schedule a 15-minute call

**Step 2:** During the call, share your screen and show them:
1. Their current website (the screenshot from the audit)
2. The Prestige Score (explain what it means)
3. The specific problems the AI found
4. Examples of the marketing assets you generated

**Step 3:** Make the offer
- "We'll build you a new website that fixes all these issues"
- "Price: $5,000 for a complete redesign"
- "Timeline: 2 weeks"
- "Payment: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery"

**Step 4:** If they say yes, collect the deposit via Stripe

## Understanding the Dashboard

### Command Center
This is your home base. It shows:
- **Total Leads:** How many businesses you've scraped
- **Audited:** How many have been analyzed by AI
- **Qualified:** How many are in your target range (40-70 score)
- **Contacted:** How many you've reached out to

**The buttons:**
- **Business Scraper:** Find new leads
- **Orchestrator:** Run audits on leads
- **Charmer:** Send outreach emails
- **System:** Technical settings (you probably won't need this)

### Business Scraper
This tool finds restaurants on Google Maps and adds them to your database.

**What you enter:**
- **Location:** The city you're targeting (e.g., "Reno, NV")
- **Query:** What type of business (e.g., "restaurants", "bars", "cafes")

**What it finds:**
- Business name
- Website URL
- Phone number
- Address

### Orchestrator
This is where the AI does its magic.

**The Pipeline has 3 stages:**
1. **Screenshot:** Takes a picture of their website
2. **Assets:** AI analyzes the website and generates marketing materials
3. **Outreach:** Prepares personalized email copy

**The "Run Pipeline" button:** Runs all 3 stages on every lead that hasn't been audited yet.

**The "Execute Pipeline" button:** Runs the pipeline on just one specific lead.

### Charmer
This is your outreach dashboard.

**What you see:**
- List of audited restaurants
- Their Prestige Score
- Status (Not Contacted, Contacted, Responded, etc.)

**What you do:**
- Click on a restaurant
- Review the AI-generated email
- Send it (or customize it first)

## The Prestige Score (What It Means)

The AI gives each website a score from 0-100 based on:
- **Visual Appeal:** Does it look modern?
- **Mobile Responsiveness:** Does it work on phones?
- **Loading Speed:** Is it fast?
- **Content Quality:** Is the copy good?
- **Call-to-Action:** Is it easy to contact them or make a reservation?

**Your Target Range: 40-70**

**Why 40-70?**
- **Under 40:** Website is SO bad they probably don't have money to fix it
- **40-70:** Website is bad enough to need help, but the business is successful enough to afford you
- **Over 70:** Website is already pretty good, they don't need you

## The Sales Call Script

### Opening (2 minutes)
"Thanks for taking the time to chat. I ran an AI audit on your website and found some interesting things. Can I share my screen and walk you through what I found?"

### The Audit Review (5 minutes)
1. Show them the screenshot of their current website
2. Show them the Prestige Score: "Your website scored a 55 out of 100. Here's what that means..."
3. Walk through the top 3 problems the AI found
4. Show them the marketing assets the AI generated: "Here's what we could do instead..."

### The Offer (3 minutes)
"Based on what I'm seeing, I'd recommend a complete redesign. We can fix all these issues and get you to an 85+ score. The investment is $5,000, and we can have it done in 2 weeks. Does that sound like something you'd be interested in?"

### Handling Objections (5 minutes)

**"That's too expensive"**
→ "I understand. Let me ask: how many customers do you think you're losing per month because your website isn't mobile-friendly? If we can get you just 10 extra reservations per month, this pays for itself in 3 months."

**"I need to think about it"**
→ "Totally fair. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the price, the timeline, or something else?"

**"Can you do it for less?"**
→ "I can do a smaller package for $3,000 that focuses on just the mobile responsiveness and loading speed. Would that work better?"

### Closing (2 minutes)
"Great! I'll send you an invoice for the 50% deposit ($2,500). Once that's paid, I'll get started immediately and have a mockup for you to review by Friday. Sound good?"

## Common Problems & Solutions

### "The scraper isn't finding any businesses"
**Fix:** Make sure you're entering a valid city name and search term. Try "Reno, Nevada" instead of just "Reno".

### "The audits are taking forever"
**Fix:** This is normal. 20 audits take about 10 minutes. Go get coffee.

### "The AI-generated email sounds robotic"
**Fix:** Edit it before sending. Add a personal touch: "I noticed you just celebrated your 10th anniversary - congrats!"

### "No one is responding to my emails"
**Fix:** 
1. Make sure you're targeting the right score range (40-70)
2. Send follow-ups after 3 days
3. Try calling them instead of just emailing

### "They want to see examples of your work"
**Fix:** Show them the marketing assets the AI generated for THEIR business. Say: "This is what I'd build for you."

### "They're asking for references"
**Fix:** First 3 clients: "I'm just launching this service, so I'm offering a discounted rate of $3,000 instead of my usual $5,000. You'd be one of my first clients."

## Red Flags (When to Walk Away)

### Don't work with clients who:

2. **Ask for "just a few tweaks" for free** - Charge for revisions after 2 rounds
3. **Don't have a budget** - If they can't afford $3k, they can't afford you
4. **Are rude or demanding** - Life's too short
