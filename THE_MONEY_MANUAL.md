## What The Hell Is This Thing?

**Velvet Alchemy** is a fully automated money-printing machine that finds local businesses with shitty websites, proves they're hemorrhaging cash, generates replacement websites with AI, and collects $3k-$8k per deal through Stripe.

Think of it as a **revenue vending machine**: put in leads, get out money.

## How It Makes You Money (The Simple Version)

### Step 1: Find Victims (I Mean "Prospects")
- Google Maps scraper finds local businesses (plumbers, dentists, lawyers, etc.)
- You already have **49 leads** loaded and ready to go
- Takes 30 seconds to scrape 50+ businesses in any city

### Step 2: Prove They're Losing Money
- AI analyzes their website and calculates **exact dollar losses**
- Example: "Your slow website is costing you $19,200/year in lost customers"
- Shows missing CTAs, broken mobile design, SSL issues, etc.
- Generates a fancy "Digital Health Grade" (A-F) that makes them feel bad

### Step 3: Generate Replacement Website (Zero Cost)
- AI creates a complete HTML/CSS/JS website in 2-3 minutes
- Industry-specific colors, mobile-responsive, contact forms, SEO
- Includes revenue loss stats to justify the price
- Download as ZIP file, ready to deliver

### Step 4: Collect The Bag 💰
- Click "Send Invoice ($5k)" button
- Stripe checkout link auto-copies to clipboard
- Send link to client via email/text
- They pay with credit card
- Money hits your bank account
- System auto-updates lead to "PAID" status

### Step 5: Deliver & Repeat
- Send them the ZIP file
- They upload to their hosting (or you charge extra to do it)
- Rinse and repeat with next lead

## Real Talk: What Can Go Wrong?

### Potential Issues:
1. **Low response rates** - Most cold emails get ignored
   - **Solution:** The audit report is your foot in the door. It's free value.

2. **Price objections** - "$5k is too much!"
   - **Solution:** Show them the revenue loss calculation. If they're losing $19k/year, $5k is a bargain.

3. **Technical delivery** - Client doesn't know how to upload files
   - **Solution:** Charge extra $500 for "white glove delivery" and use Netlify/Vercel (5 min setup)

4. **Stripe fees** - 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
   - **Solution:** Built into pricing. $5k deal = $145 in fees, you keep $4,855

5. **Refund requests** - Client changes mind
   - **Solution:** No refunds policy after website is delivered. Stated in invoice.
